### Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Where can I find my registered events?**
A: Navigate to the "Profile" tab and tap on "My Registrations". All events you have successfully registered for will appear there along with their status.

**Q: Can I unregister from an event?**
A: Yes, you can cancel your registration from the "My Registrations" screen up until 24 hours before the event begins.

**Q: Who do I contact for a refund?**
A: Refunds are handled on a case-by-case basis by the Finance Committee. Please email them directly with your transaction ID.
